# About FOBOH

We are building AI Agents for food and beverage wholesale and distribution

We partner with food and beverage manufacturers and distributors to optimise cost-to-serve by deploying AI employees trained to handle tasks that are currently labour-intensive and prone to error. 
Our first AI Agent (Employee) is a Foodservice order management agent.

## Our Mission

Building a simpler, smarter, more sustainable wholesale ecosystem

We are a profit for purpose company, and we imagine a world where food is more available & affordable. At scale we want to reduce foods in landfills and trucks on the road.
